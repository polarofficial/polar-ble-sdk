From https://www.mickf.net/tech/date-with-no-time-operations-swift A string that represents dates using their ISO 8601 representations.

PolarPlainDate is a way to handle dates with no time — such as 2022-03-02 for March 2nd of 2022 — to perform operations with convenience including adding days, dealing with ranges, etc.
